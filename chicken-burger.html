Ingredients


  • 2 large Chicken Fillets
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 Tbsp All Purpose flour
  • Salt and Black Pepper
  • 1 Tbsp sliced Jalapenos (optional but recommended!)
  • 1 tsp Olive Oil

Equipment


  • Chef's Knife
  • Chopping Board
  • Food Processor
  • Large, heavy bottomed frying pan
  • Fish slice/spatula

Method


  1. Place your pan on a medium-high heat and add your oil
  2. While the pan is heating up, roughly chop the chicken into chunks.
  3. Throw the chicken, garlic, egg, flour and Jalapenos into a food processor
  4. Pulse the ingredients until JUST about blended together.
  5. Pull out a golf-ball sized amount of the mixture and give it a squeeze to remove excess moisture
  6. Roll the mixture in between the palm of your hand until a ball forms and then squash it flat
  7. Carefully place in the hot pan and fry for 4-5 mins each side
  8. Ensure it's cooked through by pressing down on the patty and making sure the juices run clear
  9. Serve with your favourite toppings/condiments (mine are mayo, lettuce and red onion!)
