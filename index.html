Cara's Kitchen

I've always loved cooking but when my wonderful daughter Cara was born in 2015 I had to completely rethink how I approached it.

All of a sudden there was a hungry mouth with no teeth and a seemingly bottomless stomach in the house. Once she had left the bottle of milk and mushed up vegetable stage, I found myself trying to find different recipes that she would enjoy.

Some I found online, some I made up. Some were hits and some ended up as stubborn stains on the dining room wall.

I decided to write down these recipes so that one day I can share with her all her favourite recipes from growing and so Cara's Kitchen was born.

My hope is that when she's moved out and feels a bit homesick, she can look at these recipes, cook up something delicious and have a taste of home wherever she is.

Cara's still a kid as I write this so until that time comes I'm sharing these recipes with you and hoping you can get as much enjoyment out of them as she has.

Dave
