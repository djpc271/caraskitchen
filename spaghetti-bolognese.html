Ingredients


  • 500g beef mince
  • 1 each, carrot, celery, onion, finely diced
  • 200mls Passata
  • 2 cans diced tomatoes
  • 2 Tbsps Tomato puree
  • 100g mushrooms, thinly sliced
  • 1 green pepper, diced
  • 4 cloves of Garlic, finely diced
  • Salt and Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp each dried Basil, dried Oregano and dried Thyme
  • 1 Tbsp Olive Oil

Equipment


  • Chefs Knife
  • Chopping Board
  • Large heavy bottomed saucepan
  • Wooden/Silicon Spoon

Method


  1. Heat oil in saucepan on a medium heat
  2. Add celery, carrot and onion to pan and fry until onion is translucent
  3. Add in mince, stir to seperate and fry until just starting to brown
  4. Add in fresh garlic, salt & pepper, and dried herbs and stir to combine
  5. Add in mushrooms and diced green pepper, stir to combine and cook for 4-5 mins
  6. Add in the passata, diced tomatoes, tomato puree and half a cup of water. Give it all a good mix.
  7. Turn up the heat to med-high and bring to a simmer for 10 mins
  8. After 10 mins, reduce the heat to med-low and let simmer gently for 30 mins, stirring occassionally
  9. After 30 mins your sauce should have thickened nicely. Serve with your favourite pasta, preferrably spaghetti!
