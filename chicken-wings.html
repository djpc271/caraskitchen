Ingredients


  • 1 kg Chicken Wings
  • 4 Tbsps all purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 4 Tbsps Panko Breadcrumbs
  • Salt and Black Pepper
  • 1/2 tsp each Garlic Powder, Onion Powder, Paprika and Chilli Powder
  • 250ml Vegetable Oil

Equipment


  • Deep Fat Fryer
  • Large saucepan (if no deep fat fryer available)
  • Food thermometer (if no deep fat fryer available)
  • 3 bowls
  • Paper towels

Method


  1. Heat oil (in fryer or pan) to 130C
  2. Combine Flour, Garlic Powder, Onion Powder, Paprika and Chilli Powder in one bowl
  3. Beat your eggs in another bowl with a fork
  4. Dump your breadcrumbs into a 3rd bowl
  5. One at a time coat the wings in the flour, then egg, then breadcrumbs.
  6. Carefully fry the wings in the oil, about 6 at a time, for 4-7 mins, then remove and place on paper towel
  7. Turn the heat up on the oil until it reaches 180C
  8. Again 6 at a time, place the wings back in the oil for 3-4 mins until golden and crispy
  9. Remove and place on paper towels to mop up the excess oil
  10. Serve with chips and your favourite dipping sauce
